About RAB Homes Nig Ltd

Headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria — luxury real estate development & property management.

RAB Homes Nig Ltd is a luxury real estate development and property management company headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. The company was established with a vision to redefine modern living through a fusion of American architectural precision, Arabian elegance, and Italian luxury craftsmanship.

RAB Homes specializes in delivering premium residential and commercial properties that reflect superior quality, refined design, and long-term investment value. With a strong commitment to integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction, the company serves discerning clients seeking elegance, comfort, and excellence in every detail.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of Africa’s most respected luxury real estate brands, setting new benchmarks in architectural excellence, customer experience, and sustainable property development, while creating timeless spaces that inspire confidence, prosperity, and pride of ownership.
